Monthly Data Validation Tool

Automated Data Validation

Every month, data from 72 global sites had to be validated before feeding into audited investor reports for a publicly traded company. The process was entirely manual — exporting, cross-checking, and chasing corrections — taking around two days each cycle. I built a desktop app that pulls the data, runs all validations automatically, and generates a formatted report of flagged issues. What took two days now takes under an hour, and can be repeated as often as needed with zero extra effort.
