Contrast Revival

Automated Booking & Smart Lock Integration

I helped a small business move to a fully self-service model by building an app that connects their booking system to a smart lock on their premises. When a customer books, the system automatically generates a time-limited access code for the door and emails it ahead of their appointment. The result: longer operating hours, more booking capacity, and no staff needed on-site to let customers in — all for ~£5/month.

Result: Fully self-service operation running 24/7 for ~£5/month
